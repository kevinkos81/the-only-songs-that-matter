The Butterfly Collector" by The Jam is not on the standard UK or US versions of the "Setting Sons" album. It was included on the Canadian pressing of the album. It was also released as a single, backed by "Strange Town", and later appeared on compilation albums like "Snap!" and "The Jam Collection
